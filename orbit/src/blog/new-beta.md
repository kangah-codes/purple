# Purple Beta 2: Taking Things Further 🚀

A few months ago, I shared the story of building Purple — how it started as a weekend project, evolved into something more serious, and eventually launched into beta. Since then, I’ve been quietly working away on the next big update: **Purple Beta 2**.

This release brings a handful of new features and refinements that make Purple feel more complete, more useful, and honestly… a lot more fun to use.

Here’s what’s new.

---

## A fresh coat of paint 🎨

The first thing you’ll notice in Beta 2 is a **refreshed UI**. I’ve refined the color palette, updated icons, and polished various visual elements to make the app feel more cohesive and modern. The goal was to create a more inviting and enjoyable experience without straying too far from the original design.

![Recurring transactions](/blog/beta-2/refresh.png)

![Recurring transactions](/blog/beta-2/refresh-2.png)

---

## Recurring Transactions 🔁

Got subscriptions, bills, or regular income? You can now set up **recurring transactions** that automatically populate your transaction list on schedule.

Once configured, Purple takes care of creating those entries for you. Monthly subscriptions, weekly expenses, salary payments, you name it. This removes one of the biggest sources of friction in budgeting: repeatedly entering the same transactions over and over again.

It’s a small feature on the surface, but it makes day-to-day usage _much_ smoother.

![Recurring transactions](/blog/beta-2/recurring-transactions.png)

---

## Revamped Budgets 📝

The UX of the previous plan implementation was fairly clunky and far from ideal. To set budget limits for specific categories, you had to create separate plans for each individual category. As you can imagine, if you had broad spending habits, this quickly meant managing multiple plans recreated every month just to cover your typical expenses.

This new version takes a completely different approach by introducing new budget types: fixed budgets, with flexible budgets coming in a later feature drop. You can now create a single budget that spans multiple categories, allocate limits to each, and track your spending against those limits in a far more intuitive way. You’ll also receive timely notifications as you approach or exceed your budget, helping you stay in control and on track.

![Recurring transactions](/blog/beta-2/budgets.png)

## Better Reports with Month Filtering 📊

Reports are only useful if you can actually _slice_ your data in meaningful ways.

In Beta 2, reports now support **month-based filtering**, allowing you to:

-   Review a specific month in isolation
-   Compare spending patterns across different periods
-   Spot trends without noise from older data

This makes reports far more actionable, especially if you regularly review your finances month by month.

![Recurring transactions](/blog/beta-2/monthly-report.png)

---

## Net Worth Tracking 📈

One of the most requested features is finally here: **net worth tracking**.

Purple now lets you see the bigger picture by combining:

-   Your accounts
-   Your balances
-   Your overall financial position over time

It’s not just about budgeting anymore, it’s about understanding where you stand financially and how that changes as you make decisions.

![Recurring transactions](/blog/beta-2/net-worth.png)

## Some more goodies 🎁

-   Experimental local backup and restore using device storage - Users can now back up their data locally to their device storage and restore from it if needed. This is an early version of backup functionality, with cloud backup planned for a future release.
-   App log exports - Users can export app logs to help diagnose issues when reporting bugs.
-   OTA update improvements - Users can now receive OTA updates more reliably, with fewer interruptions.
-   Various bug fixes and performance improvements - A number of smaller bugs have been squashed, and overall app performance has been improved for a smoother experience.
-   Refined onboarding UX with clearer instructions and visuals to help new users get started quickly.

---

## Known Issues

As with any beta release, there are still some rough edges. Here are a few known issues:

-   Some users may experience occasional extended load times when opening the app. This is being actively investigated. For a temporary workaround, try closing and reopening the app.
-   Some users may have recurring transactions not being recorded consistently. If you encounter this, please report it on our [roadmap page](https://purpleapp.featurebase.app/roadmap) so it can be prioritized for a fix.
-   Some users may notice transactions screen does not allow scrolling even when there are many transactions. A fix is in the works for this issue.
-   Pinned accounts are not consistently displayed first on the accounts carousel on the home screen. This is being addressed in an upcoming patch.
-   Some users may experience stuttering animations when swiping between certain screens. Performance optimizations are planned to resolve this.
